The all-new chip P9

Pine R&D Department develop a custom Pine silicon makes PineWatch Series 9 more capable, intuitive and faster. The new dual-core CPU has 8.7 billion transistors — 70 per cent more than the P8 chip. The brand new P9 processes machine learning tasks up to two times faster. It enables a number of innovations, including a new gesture — clench fist.

Health care

PineWatch helps you better understand your mental and physical health. PineWatch has powerful apps
that make it the best device for healthy life. PineWatch always checks for unusually high or low heart rate
in background, and send notification to user to prevent serious underlying condition. Beside that, when a
hard fall is detacted with PineWatch, an alert appears and allow user to easily call emergency services.
